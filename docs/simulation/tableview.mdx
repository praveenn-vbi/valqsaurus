1. By clicking on the table icon at the top of the Simulate tab canvas.

## 9. Customize columns

Click the Settings icon at the top right corner, then click **Settings** option.
On the left, search/select or unselect the columns that you want to display.
On the right, you can reorder columns by drag and drop. 

Click **Reset to default** to erase the changes and go back to the default settings.
