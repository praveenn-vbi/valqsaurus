Access/Update Plan

In this section, you'll learn to access Plan tab and update an existing plan using the Lock/ Unlock features on ValQ

#Access Plan

Click on the Plan icon as highlighted in the image below to open the Plan tab. Before creating a new plan, ValQ allows you to Lock your model on the Plan tab screen. Locking your model ensures the following:

  • Converts the dynamic nodes in your model to nodes
  • The structure of your model, the nodes and configuration on it are fixed while you're planning
In this regard, ValQ gives you a warning message saying 'Any changes made in the model will re-calculate all the allocations in each series and archive the previous steps'. Therefore, we recommend you to completely build your model before you begin to plan.

Once you are ready to Plan, click the Lock button.


Note

In case you want to make changes to your model while planning, you can unlock your model at any time. Click here to learn to unlock your model.

After locking the model, you will now be able to:

  • Name the series under Series Name text box
  • Choose the series to start with from the drop-down list
  • Select the period for which you want to plan from Periods option
