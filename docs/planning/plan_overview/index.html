Plan Overview

#What's Plan?

Plan's easy-to-use interface enables you to do your planning, budgeting and forecasting all at one place instantly on ValQ to achieve your business goals.

The most common use cases of the Plan tab are as follows:

  • Create a new plan/budget based on an existing data series
  • Create a new plan from scratch (Zero-based Budgeting)
  • Perform Time Series Forecasting
  • Update an existing plan/forecast by leveraging various allocation methods
  • Plan, Budget and Forecast based on Key Values Drivers
  • Plan continuously by creating a rolling forecast
  • Use the plan as a series for Scenario analysis and Variance analysis

#Pre-requisite

At least one Data Series and a ValQ model is all you need to Plan

#Why use Plan?

Planning, budgeting and forecasting are the main elements of a company's performance management framework. ValQ provides a self-service solution and common platform - 'Plan' to execute and monitor these functions enabling real-time reporting & analytics.

Plan supports quick, accurate and reliable decision making to meet the strategic and operational needs of your business. With ValQ's intelligent plan grid you can perform all areas of organizational planning including financial planning and analysis, sales planning, supply chain planning, workforce planning and marketing planning.
