Commenting

Collaboration helps to share your model changes, simulations and comments to all the users inside the same workspace. You have to Upload your model before collaborating with other users. Assuming a workspace member simulates on the existing scenario, creates a new scenario, deletes another node, comments on the changes made and saves the model.


ValQ Collaborate lets you to view the member's changes, simulations and comments and to make some own changes to the model.

The comments between the users are displayed at a node level under the Comments icon. The changes at the model level are captured at the History Log.

Comments#

Add comments for Planning#

To add a comment for Plan tab, select the cell you want to comment on and then click the comment option. After posting, your comment is then saved to the comment section for Planning under the planning series and node hierarchy alongside your username and time stamp.

Add comments for Simulation#

On the Simulate canvas, you can comment on the changes you have made by clicking the comment icon on top of the node which appears as you hover over the node. After posting, your comment is then saved to the comment section for Simulation under the scenario name and node hierarchy alongside your username and time stamp.


Alternatively, you can check the comment log for the entire model by clicking the comments icon at the bezel. Comments are displayed at the node level for Planning and Simulation.

View, Edit, Delete Comments#

The comments can be edited anytime by using the pencil icon next to your name. The bin icon can be used to delete a comment permanently. The users part of the same workspace will be able to view the comments made by other members and can add their comments to the model.

History Log#

As you go to the 3 dots ... at the bezel and click History Log, you can see the changes made to the model in chronological order for Planning and Simulation. This feature helps with auditing the ValQ model.
